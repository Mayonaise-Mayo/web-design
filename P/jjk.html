About Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Gege Akutami. The story follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student who becomes involved in the world of jujutsu sorcery after he consumes a cursed object—a finger belonging to the powerful Curse, Ryomen Sukuna. Yuji joins the Tokyo Prefectural Jujutsu High School to learn how to fight Curses and protect the world from malevolent spirits.

Main Characters

Yuji Itadori

The protagonist of the series, Yuji possesses immense physical strength and a strong sense of justice. After consuming Sukuna's finger, he becomes the vessel for the King of Curses.

Megumi Fushiguro

A first-year student at Jujutsu High, Megumi is a talented sorcerer who uses Shikigami techniques to summon powerful familiars to fight Curses.

Nobara Kugisaki

A confident and brash first-year student, Nobara uses her Straw Doll Technique to manipulate Curses and destroy them with precision.

Satoru Gojo

The strongest jujutsu sorcerer, Gojo is a teacher at Jujutsu High. He possesses immense power, especially with his Limitless and Infinity techniques.

Where should you start?

  • Manga: Start with Volume 1, where the story begins.
  • Anime: Watch the anime adaptation produced by MAPPA, which covers major arcs of the series.
  • Jujutsu Kaisen 0: A prequel movie that explores the events before the main storyline.

Side Characters

Toji Fushiguro

He was a former member of the Zenin clan and the infamous assassin known as the Sorcerer Killer (術じゅつ師し殺ごろし Jutsushi Goroshi).

Maki

Born a non-sorcerer in one of the Big Three Sorcerer Families, Maki was constantly mistreated and felt compelled to expel herself from the Zenin clan. She aspires to be a great jujutsu sorcerer to spite her family.
